OpenBlox Maps UniStore

This UniStore provides downloadable classic-style Roblox maps (.rbxl) for use with OpenBlox DS. All maps are installable directly to your SD card using Universal-Updater.

UniStore URL:
https://aspenini.github.io/openblox-unistore/openblox.unistore
How to Use:
  1. Open Universal-Updater on your 3DS
  2. Scan or enter the UniStore URL above
  3. Browse and install OpenBlox maps
  4. Maps will be placed in sdmc:/OpenBLOX/ automatically
